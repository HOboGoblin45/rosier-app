Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Rosier ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, website, and services (collectively, the "Services").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, Instagram handle (optional), name, age, and profile preferences
  • Fashion Preferences: Swipe history, saved items, wish lists, and style profile data
  • Contact Information: Email address for communications, notifications, and customer support
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through third-party payment processors (we do not directly store credit card information)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • Usage Data: App interactions, swipes, saves, searches, browsing history, and time spent in the app
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (used for localized content and analytics only)
  • Log Data: Server logs including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies, pixels, and similar tracking technologies for analytics and personalization

2.3 Third-Party Information

If you link your Instagram account, we may collect limited profile information with your permission. We do not store your Instagram password.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Services
  • To personalize your experience and deliver custom recommendations through our Style DNA engine
  • To process transactions and send transactional emails
  • To send promotional emails and marketing communications (which you can opt out of)
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity and abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
  • To respond to your inquiries and customer support requests

4. Style DNA and AI Personalization

Our Style DNA engine learns from your swipe behavior, saves, and purchases to provide personalized fashion recommendations. This data is:

  • Processed on secure servers with encryption
  • Used to improve recommendations for your account only
  • Never sold to third parties as individual profiles
  • Subject to aggregate analytics for product improvement
  • Retained as long as your account exists, unless you request deletion

5. How We Share Your Information

5.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share information with trusted service providers who assist in operating our Services, including:

  • Cloud hosting providers (Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud)
  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel)
  • Email services (Listmonk, Sendgrid)
  • Payment processors (Stripe)
  • Affiliate tracking partners (for retailer commissions)

5.2 Retail Partners

When you purchase items through our app, we may share your purchase data with retail partners for fulfillment and affiliate tracking purposes only. This does not constitute a sale of personal information.

5.3 Legal Disclosure

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements
  • Protect the safety and security of our users
  • Protect our rights and property

5.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • Secure authentication and password hashing
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and role-based permissions for team members

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured format)
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

7.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale or sharing of personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information collected from you
  • Correct inaccurate personal information

7.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@rosier.app. We will respond to your request within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Track usage patterns and analytics
  • Deliver personalized content
  • Prevent fraud

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling cookies may impact app functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will immediately delete such information and terminate the child's account.

10. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Services and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@rosier.app.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from your home country. By using Rosier, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries other than your country of residence.

12. Third-Party Links

Our app contains links to third-party websites and services, including retail partners and social media platforms. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party services, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing your information.

13. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - Do Not Sell My Information

Rosier does not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA. We share information with service providers for business purposes only (such as analytics, payment processing, and affiliate tracking). If you have questions about how your information is shared, please contact us.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or by posting a notice on our website. Your continued use of the Services after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
