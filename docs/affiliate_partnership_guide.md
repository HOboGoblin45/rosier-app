## Section 1: Revenue Potential Analysis

### Affiliate Revenue Calculator

**Conservative Scenario (Month 1-2):**
- Users: 5,000 (launch cohort)
- CTR (click-through rate): 2%
- Conversion rate: 2% (from retailer landing page)
- AOV (average order value): $200
- Commission rate: 10% (average across networks)
- **Monthly Revenue: $400** (5,000 × 2% × 2% × $200 × 10%)

**Growth Scenario (Month 3-6):**
- Users: 25,000 (organic growth + marketing)
- CTR: 5% (improved product placement + engagement)
- Conversion rate: 3% (improved retailer experience)
- AOV: $150 (mix of price points)
- Commission rate: 10%
- **Monthly Revenue: $5,625** (25,000 × 5% × 3% × $150 × 10%)

**Target Scenario (Month 6+):**
- Users: 50,000
- CTR: 8% (optimized, curated selections)
- Conversion rate: 4% (user trust + repeat behavior)
- AOV: $180
- Commission rate: 10%
- **Monthly Revenue: $28,800** (50,000 × 8% × 4% × $180 × 10%)

### Comparison: Affiliate vs. Alternative Revenue Paths

| Revenue Path | Timeline to $10K MRR | Difficulty | Scalability |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Affiliate Networks** | 5-7 months | Low (networks do heavy lifting) | High (scales with user base) |
| **Direct Brand Deals** | 8-12 months | Medium (requires BD work) | Medium (limited # of deals) |
| **Data Products** | 12+ months | High (requires infrastructure) | Medium (niche market) |
| **Sponsored Wallpapers** | 6-8 months | Medium (requires sales team) | Low (limited inventory) |

### **Answer: YES, affiliate is the fastest path to revenue.**

**Why?**
1. **No inventory risk** — You don't hold product
2. **Immediate integration** — Networks provide tracking and payouts
3. **Scalable with users** — Revenue grows as app grows
4. **Low operational overhead** — No customer service or returns management
5. **Easy testing** — Can A/B test commission rates and product placement
6. **Quick approval** — Network affiliations can be approved in 2-7 days

**Timeline to $10K MRR:**
- Month 1: Apply to networks + set up tracking
- Month 2-3: First affiliate revenue (likely $500-$2,000)
- Month 4-5: Optimization phase, reach $5K-$7K MRR
- Month 6: Scale to $10K+ MRR with optimized placement

## Key Success Factors

1. **Network Affiliate Links First** — Don't wait for direct brand deals. Networks give you 80% of brands with 5% of the work.

2. **Optimize for CTR** — Fashion apps typically see 2-8% CTR. Rosier should target 5%+ through:
   - Prominent product placement (hero sections)
   - Curated collections over random products
   - Social proof (saves, likes, trending)
   - Clear "Shop Now" CTAs

3. **Convert on Retailer Pages** — You control traffic, not conversion. Optimize:
   - Seasonal products (summer collection launch)
   - New arrivals (better conversion)
   - Price points ($150-$300 sweet spot)
   - Cart abandonment (cross-brand merchandising)

4. **Diversify Revenue** — Don't rely on one retailer:
   - Target 60% from networks (SSENSE, NET-A-PORTER, Farfetch)
   - Target 30% from secondary retailers (FWRD, Browns, Shopbop)
   - Target 10% from brand partnerships

5. **Track & Measure Everything** — Success depends on:
   - Daily cohort analysis (which users convert)
   - Product performance (which items get clicks/sales)
   - Retailer comparison (NET-A-PORTER vs. SSENSE conversion rates)
   - Seasonal trends (spring/summer vs. fall/winter)
