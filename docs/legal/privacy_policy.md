**Usage Data:**

- First-party cookies for session management

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 3.1 No Sale of Personal Data

Rosier does not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We do not share your email address, account identifier, or personal account data with external partners or retailers.

### 3.2 Aggregated and Anonymized Data

We may share aggregated and anonymized data with brand partners and retailers for business purposes, including:

**Trend Analysis:**
- Consumer preference trends (e.g., "80% of Rosier users prefer minimalist footwear")
- Category-level analytics (e.g., luxury brands, sustainable fashion)
- Seasonal trends and demand forecasting

**Minimum Cohort Protection:**
- Data is only shared in aggregates of at least 50 users
- Individual user behavior cannot be identified or inferred
- No personal identifiers are included in shared reports

**Data Recipients:**
- Fashion brands and designers
- Retailers and affiliate partners
- Market research firms
- Industry analysts

### 3.3 Service Providers

We share information with service providers who assist us in operating the Service:

- **Cloud Infrastructure:** Amazon Web Services (AWS)
- **Analytics:** Firebase, Amplitude, Mixpanel
- **Error Tracking:** Sentry
- **Email Services:** SendGrid, Mailgun
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe, Apple, Google
- **Affiliate Networks:** Rakuten, Impact, Awin, Skimlinks

Service providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as necessary to provide services and must maintain appropriate data security.

### 3.4 Affiliate Networks

When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase:

- **Affiliate Tracking:** The affiliate network (Rakuten, Impact, Awin, etc.) receives information that you clicked the link and made a purchase
- **Commission:** We receive a commission notification from the affiliate network
- **Privacy:** We do not receive your payment information, shipping address, or detailed purchase history
- **Affiliate Privacy:** Your interaction with the affiliate network is governed by their privacy policy

### 3.5 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or legal process:

- Subpoenas or court orders
- Government investigations
- National security requests
- Protection of legal rights or safety
- Compliance with applicable regulations

### 3.6 Business Transfers

If Rosier is acquired, merged, or substantially reorganized, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change and provide you with the opportunity to opt out if required by law.

## 7. CCPA-Specific Provisions (California Residents)

### 7.1 Categories of Personal Information Collected

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), we collect the following categories of personal information:

- **Identifiers:** Name, email, phone, device identifiers
- **Commercial Information:** Products viewed, purchased, shopping activity
- **Internet Activity:** Browsing history, clicks, interactions
- **Device Information:** Device type, OS, unique identifiers
- **Geolocation:** Approximate location, GPS coordinates
- **Inferences:** Preferences, characteristics, trends

### 7.2 "Do Not Sell My Personal Information"

Rosier does not sell your personal information in the traditional sense. However, under California law, targeted advertising and sharing with analytics providers may constitute a "sale" of personal information.

You have the right to opt out of this activity through:
1. **App Settings:** Account > Privacy > "Do Not Sell My Personal Information"
2. **Email:** privacy@rosier.app with subject line "Do Not Sell My Personal Information"
3. **Authorized Agent:** Submit a request on your behalf with power of attorney

### 7.3 Your CCPA Rights

As a California resident, you have the following rights:

**Right to Know:** Request what personal information we collect, use, and disclose.

**Right to Delete:** Request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to exceptions.

**Right to Opt Out:** Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information.

**Right to Correct:** Request correction of inaccurate personal information.

**Right to Limit Use:** Limit our use of sensitive personal information to necessary purposes.

To exercise these rights:
- Email: privacy@rosier.app
- Portal: https://rosier.app/privacy/california
- Phone: Contact customer support for verification assistance

### 7.4 No Discrimination

Rosier will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

### 7.5 Shine the Light

Under California's "Shine the Light" law, you may request information about third parties with whom we share personal information for their own marketing purposes. Rosier does not share personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes, but you may contact us to confirm.

**Analytics Cookies:**

- Feature usage tracking
- Performance monitoring

**Preference Cookies:**

### 9.2 Cookie Management
