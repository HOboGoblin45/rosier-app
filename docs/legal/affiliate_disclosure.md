# Affiliate Disclosure

**Rosier Inc. - Affiliate Link Disclosure**

Last Updated: April 2026

## Clear Affiliate Relationship Statement

**Rosier earns affiliate commissions when you make purchases through links in our Service.** This disclosure outlines the nature of our affiliate relationships and how they work.

## How Affiliate Links Work

### What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special links that track when you click them and complete a purchase. When you:

1. Click a product link in Rosier
2. Are directed to a retailer's website
3. Complete a purchase on that retailer's site

The retailer recognizes the traffic came from Rosier and pays us a commission on your purchase.

### Commission Structure

- **Commission Amount:** Varies by retailer (typically 3-15% of your purchase)
- **Your Price:** Not affected - you pay the same price you would if you visited the retailer directly
- **Our Revenue:** Commissions are our primary revenue source and allow us to operate the Service
- **Retailer Payment:** The retailer pays the commission, not you

- **Rakuten Advertising** - Multi-retailer affiliate network

## No Influence on Product Selection

### Editorial Independence

The inclusion of products in Rosier is determined by our editorial standards for niche fashion, not by commission rates. Specifically:

- **No Commission Bias:** Higher-commission products are not prioritized over lower-commission ones
- **Consistent Curation:** We maintain the same editorial standards regardless of affiliate terms
- **Diverse Partners:** We feature products from retailers with varying commission rates
- **User-Focused:** Recommendations prioritize user preferences, not commission potential

### Our Selection Criteria

Products are selected based on:
- Niche fashion quality and aesthetic
- Brand reputation and authenticity
- User preference data
- Trend analysis
- Editorial curation by our team

Commission rates are not a factor in these decisions.

## Affiliate Network Tracking

### How Tracking Works

When you click an affiliate link:

1. **Network Tracking Cookie:** The affiliate network sets a tracking cookie on your device
2. **Network Identification:** The network identifies your click and purchase
3. **Commission Attribution:** The purchase is attributed to Rosier
4. **Data Sharing:** The network may track your activity across other sites using their network

### Data Shared with Affiliate Networks

Through affiliate tracking, networks may receive:

- The fact that you clicked a Rosier link
- Your subsequent purchase (what you bought, price paid)
- Device and browser information
- Purchase completion and value

**Important:** Rosier does not receive your personal information (email, address, name) from affiliate networks. We only receive commission notifications and aggregate data.

### Affiliate Network Privacy

Each affiliate network has its own privacy policy:

- **Rakuten:** rakuten.com/privacy
- **Impact:** impact.com/privacy-policy
- **Awin:** awin.com/privacy-policy
- **Skimlinks:** skimlinks.com/privacy-policy

We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how they use your information.

## Cookies and Tracking

### Affiliate Cookies

Affiliate networks use cookies to track your journey from Rosier to the retailer:

- **Cookie Duration:** Typically 30-90 days (varies by network)
- **Cross-Site Tracking:** Networks may track your activity across multiple sites
- **Re-targeting:** You may see ads from retailers you visited through our links
- **Attribution:** Enables proper attribution of purchases to Rosier

### Your Control

You can control tracking through:

- **Browser Settings:** Block or delete cookies
- **App Settings:** Disable tracking in privacy preferences
- **Cookie Policies:** Visit affiliate networks' sites to opt out of their tracking
- **Device Settings:** Limit ad tracking on iOS or Android

## Questions About Affiliate Links

### Are Affiliate Links Safe?

Yes. Affiliate links are standard in the fashion and e-commerce industry:

- Your purchase experience is identical to visiting directly
- You benefit from the same prices and terms
- No personal information is exposed
- The link simply identifies the traffic source

### Will Prices Be Higher?

No. Affiliate links do not affect the price you pay. The commission comes from the retailer's marketing budget, not your purchase price.

### Can I Shop Without Using Affiliate Links?

Yes. While we encourage using links in Rosier to support us, you may:

- Visit retailers directly by name
- Search for products independently
- Find the same prices without affiliate tracking
- Browse without affiliate relationships

However, by using Rosier's links, you help support our continued operations and development of the Service.
