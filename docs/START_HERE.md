## MOST IMPORTANT: THE REFERRAL SYSTEM

Everything hinges on getting viral coefficient to 0.3+ (each user brings 0.3-0.4 new users).

**How to make this work:**
1. Make referral button PROMINENT (home screen, always visible)
2. Make sharing FRICTIONLESS (1 tap to share via iMessage, WhatsApp, etc)
3. Make rewards COMPELLING (1 invite = unlock Style DNA share, which is shareable itself = exponential)
4. Track everything (know which friends convert, which don't)

If referral coefficient is low by Week 2:
- Audit share friction: Does it take too many taps?
- Audit rewards: Do users care about unlocking features?
- Audit messaging: Is the ask clear ("Invite friends, unlock this")?
- Iterate FAST

## RED FLAGS (Things That Signal You Need to Pivot)

| Red Flag | What to Do |
|----------|-----------|
| <500 downloads week 1 | Product Hunt launch failed. What went wrong? Messaging? Timing? Review launch_social_content.md and relaunch on Reddit/TikTok. |
| <0.1 viral coefficient | Users aren't inviting. Make referral mechanics simpler, rewards more compelling, or share friction lower. |
| <3% email open rate | Subject lines aren't compelling. Test 5 new subject lines. Review copy. |
| <2% TikTok engagement | Videos aren't resonating. Post more often, test different hooks, watch what's trending. |
| >2% unsubscribe rate | You're emailing too much or wrong audience. Cut email frequency, segment list better. |
