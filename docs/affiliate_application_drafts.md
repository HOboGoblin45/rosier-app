## Success Tips

1. **Be Specific** — Include your app name, store link, and audience details.
   Generic applications get rejected.

2. **Show Data** — Even if you don't have conversion data yet, show projected
   traffic and user demographics.

3. **Be Professional** — Spell-check everything. These are business partnerships.

4. **Follow Up** — If you don't hear back in 5 business days, send a follow-up
   email.

5. **Diversify** — Don't rely on one retailer. Apply to at least 5-10 programs.

6. **Track Everything** — Keep a spreadsheet of applications, approval dates,
   and contact info for future negotiations.
