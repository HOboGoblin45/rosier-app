## What You Need to Know

### About Affiliate Networks

They're the intermediary between you and retailers. ONE network signup gives you access to 100+ brands.

Think of it like this:
- Without networks: Apply to 100 retailers individually (200 hours)
- With networks: Apply to 4 networks (1 hour), get access to 200+ retailers

**That's why we apply to networks first.**

### Commission Rates

Affiliate links earn you 5-10% commission on the sale price (excluding shipping, taxes, returns).

- $200 purchase × 7% commission = $14
- 1,000 sales/month × $14 = $14,000 revenue

(This is why we need high CTR and conversion rate.)

### How It Works (Technical)

1. User sees product in Rosier app
2. User clicks "Shop Now"
3. Click goes through affiliate link (tracks YOU as the source)
4. User lands on retailer page
5. User makes purchase
6. Retailer credits commission to your account
7. You get paid monthly

All automated. You don't touch anything except the affiliate links.

## FAQ

**Q: How long until first revenue?**
A: 30-45 days. Applications take 5-10 days, integration takes 10-15 days, first conversions start trickling in by Week 4.

**Q: What if I get rejected?**
A: Unlikely. Networks approve 95%+ of applications. Retailers approve 85%+. If you do get rejected, reasons are usually: low traffic estimate, unprofessional website, no history. You can reapply in 30-60 days.

**Q: Do I have to apply to all 4 networks?**
A: Yes. Each network has different retailers and different commission rates. Together they cover 95%+ of your options.

**Q: What's the difference between networks and direct retailers?**
A: Networks (Rakuten, Skimlinks, etc.) are intermediaries. They handle multiple retailers. Direct retailers (SSENSE, NET-A-PORTER) you apply to individually. Apply to networks FIRST because one signup = 100+ retailers.

**Q: How much time will this take ongoing?**
A: Minimal. Set up affiliate links once, they work forever. Optimization is ongoing: 2-3 hours/week analyzing metrics and testing placements.

**Q: Can I negotiate higher commission rates?**
A: Yes. Once you hit volume targets (5K+ clicks/month), you can ask for 8-10% instead of 5-7%. Networks and retailers want to incentivize top performers.

**Q: What if a retailer goes down or product sells out?**
A: The affiliate link still works, but it will show a 404 or out-of-stock page. You can't control this. Just make sure your product feed updates automatically so you're not featuring old inventory.

**Q: Do I need to disclose that I earn affiliate commission?**
A: Yes, for FTC compliance. Add a disclosure like "Rosier earns commissions on products purchased through affiliate links." Check `/docs/legal/affiliate_disclosure.md` for language.
