# Rosier SEO & Content Marketing Strategy

**Target:** 50K-100K organic monthly visits by month 6; establish Rosier as fashion discovery authority
**Timeline:** Ongoing (pre-launch through month 6+)
**Primary Channel:** Blog + landing page optimization
**Secondary:** App Store Optimization (ASO)

---

## Executive Summary

Rosier's SEO strategy focuses on two pillars:
1. **Blog Authority:** Long-form, SEO-optimized content targeting micro-influencer fashion and contemporary brand discovery (low-competition, high-intent keywords)
2. **App Store Optimization (ASO):** Keyword-rich titles, screenshots, and preview videos to drive App Store rankings and organic installs

The strategy prioritizes long-tail keywords with lower competition but high user intent (women searching for style guidance, contemporary brand discovery, etc.). Target keywords have 100-1000 monthly searches and conversion-heavy intents.

### 1.3 On-Page SEO Optimization (Technical)

**Every Blog Post Must Include:**

1. **Meta Tags**
   - Title: Include primary keyword + brand (50-60 characters)
     - Example: "Niche Fashion Discovery: Guide to Finding Your Style | Rosier"
   - Meta Description: Call-to-action + keyword (150-160 characters)
     - Example: "Learn how to discover niche contemporary fashion brands. Complete guide to style DNA, micro-influencers, and where to shop."
   - H1 Tag: Primary keyword
   - H2/H3: Secondary keywords + question format (improves featured snippet chance)

2. **Internal Linking**
   - Link to 2-3 other blog posts (contextually relevant)
   - Link to landing page (1-2 times, in footer or natural context)
   - Link to app store page (1-2 times, in CTA sections)
   - Link to referral program page (1-2 times, in growth-focused posts)

3. **Keyword Distribution**
   - Primary keyword: 1-1.5% density (use naturally, not forced)
   - H1 tags: 1 per article
   - H2 tags: 3-5 per article (include secondary keywords)
   - First 100 words: Include primary keyword by sentence 2-3
   - Image alt text: Keyword-rich but descriptive

4. **Media Optimization**
   - 1 hero image per post (high-quality, on-brand)
   - 2-3 additional images throughout
   - All images: Compressed (tools: TinyPNG, ImageOptim), descriptive alt text
   - Consider video embeds (YouTube embeds help with SEO)

5. **Readability**
   - Average paragraph: 3-4 sentences
   - Average sentence: 15-20 words
   - Subheadings: Every 300-400 words
   - Bullet points: 2-3 per section (break up dense text)
   - Word count: 2,000-3,500 words (sweet spot for fashion content)

6. **Calls-to-Action (CTAs)**
   - Early CTA (paragraph 3-4): Soft (informational) → "Download our free style guide"
   - Mid CTA (middle of post): Product-focused → "Discover similar brands in Rosier"
   - End CTA (final section): Strong conversion → "Join the waitlist for early access"

### 1.4 Blog Infrastructure & Hosting

**Setup Options:**

**Option A: Ghost (Recommended for Modern Publishing)**
- Cost: $9/month (self-hosted) or $29+/month (Ghost Pro)
- Benefits: Clean, modern design; built-in SEO; email newsletter integration; fast loading
- Setup: Deploy on Ghost Pro or self-host on DigitalOcean ($12/month)
- Integration: Can embed waitlist form, referral links, app download buttons

**Option B: Webflow (Design-First)**
- Cost: $240-745+/year (Designer/Business plan)
- Benefits: Beautiful design; full control; built-in SEO; CMS
- Setup: Faster iteration on design; integrated with Rosier brand
- Integration: Seamless with landing page (same platform)

**Option C: Notion + Super.so (Budget-Friendly)**
- Cost: Free (Notion) + $120/year (Super.so)
- Benefits: Fast setup; minimal technical knowledge
- Limitation: Less design flexibility; slower SEO benefits
- Setup: 2-3 hours to configure

**Recommendation:** Webflow (if budget allows) or Ghost Pro (best ROI for SEO content). Avoid WordPress (slow, security headaches).

### 1.5 Blog Traffic Attribution & Analytics

**Setup PostHog + Plausible:**
- **Plausible** for web analytics: Track blog traffic, CTR to app, email signups (GDPR-compliant)
- **PostHog** for in-app analytics: Track which blog posts drive highest-quality users (conversion, retention, LTV)

**Key Metrics to Track:**
- Monthly organic traffic to each blog post
- Average time on page
- Click-through rate to landing page / app store
- Bounce rate
- Conversion rate (sign-up, download, referral)
- Return visitor rate

**Monthly Review (Every 30 Days):**

- Identify underperforming posts (under 50 organic visits/month)
- Repurpose top posts into social content, email, video
- Refresh underperforming posts (update data, add video, add CTA)

## Part 2: App Store Optimization (ASO)

### 2.1 App Store Listing Strategy

**iOS App Store:**

1. **App Title (Max 30 characters)**
   - Primary: "Rosier - Swipe Fashion"
   - Alternative: "Rosier Fashion Discovery"
   - **Why:** "Swipe" signals unique UX; "Fashion" is primary keyword; 30-char limit forces clarity
   - **Keyword inclusion:** Swipe (behavioral), fashion (primary category), discovery (intent)

2. **Subtitle (Max 30 characters)**
   - "Discover Your Style DNA"
   - **Why:** Introduces unique value prop; differentiates from generic "fashion app"
   - **Keyword inclusion:** Style DNA (brand positioning), discover (intent)

3. **Keywords (Comma-separated, max 100 characters)**
   - `swipe fashion, style DNA, fashion discovery, contemporary brands, niche fashion, designer clothes, micro-influencer, fashion app, style app, clothing app`
   - **Strategy:** Mix high-volume ("fashion app") + niche ("micro-influencer", "style DNA") + competitor ("swipe", vs. Tinder for clothing apps)
   - **Character allocation:**
     - High-intent: "fashion discovery" (15 char) + "style DNA" (9 char) + "niche fashion" (13 char)
     - Discovery: "swipe fashion" (13 char) + "contemporary brands" (19 char)
     - Category: "fashion app" (11 char) + "clothing app" (12 char)
     - Influencer angle: "micro-influencer" (16 char)

4. **App Description (4,000 characters max)**
   - **Structure:** Problem → Solution → Features → Social Proof → CTA
   - **Word count:** 1,000-1,200 words (use full character limit)
   - **Sample outline:**
     ```
     [Opening Hook - Problem]
     "Finding niche fashion that matches YOUR taste is hard.
     TikTok shows you trends, but not curated pieces.
     SSENSE and Farfetch have everything, but recommendations feel impersonal.
     Rosier fixes this."

     [Solution]
     "Rosier is the fashion discovery app designed for women who follow micro-influencers,
     love contemporary brands, and want personalized recommendations without the algorithm overwhelm."

     [3-4 Features]
     "Swipe to Discover: Like Tinder for fashion. Swipe through 50,000+ pieces from
     50+ niche contemporary brands in seconds. Find your next favorite piece.

     Your Style DNA: Our AI learns your taste with every swipe.
     The more you swipe, the better our recommendations get. No 2 feeds are the same.

     Price Alerts: Save an item and set a price alert.
     Never miss a sale on pieces you love.

     Micro-Influencer Curation: Discover brands and styles trending among creators you follow,
     not just algorithm-driven trends."

     [Social Proof]
     "2,000+ women in the waitlist before launch.
     Featured in [Press mentions, if applicable]"

     [CTA]
     "Download Rosier now. Summer 2026 launch is here."
     ```
   - **Keyword distribution:** Include primary keywords (fashion discovery, style DNA, niche fashion, contemporary) naturally throughout

5. **Screenshots (5-10 images, 1242 x 2208px for iPhone 6.5")**
   - **Screenshot 1-2:** Swipe mechanics (hero feature)
     - Text overlay: "Swipe to discover niche fashion"
   - **Screenshot 3:** Style DNA / personalization
     - Text: "AI learns your taste"
   - **Screenshot 4:** Price alerts
     - Text: "Never miss a sale"
   - **Screenshot 5:** Brands / curation
     - Text: "50+ contemporary brands curated"
   - **Screenshot 6-7:** Social proof (optional)
     - Testimonials, waitlist count, press mentions

   - **Design tips:**
     - Use Rosier brand colors (gold + dark navy)
     - Show real app UI in first 2 screenshots (users expect this)
     - Overlay text large (readable on thumbnail)
     - Call-to-action on final screenshot

6. **Preview Video (15-30 seconds)**
   - **Content:** Swipe mechanics (5 sec) → Style DNA explanation (5 sec) → Price alerts (5 sec) → Brand curation (5 sec)
   - **Audio:** Upbeat contemporary music; text overlay ("Swipe", "Learn", "Save", "Discover")
   - **Voiceover:** Optional female voice (conversational, not robotic)
   - **Format:** 1080 x 1920px (vertical), no black bars
   - **Target:** 80% completion rate on App Store

7. **Release Notes (Max 170 characters)**
   - "Rosier is here! Swipe to discover niche fashion. Tap to open and start discovering."
   - **Why:** Update app store algorithm (app appears in "Updates" section); drive re-engagement

8. **Category & Subcategory**
   - Primary: Shopping
   - Subcategory: Fashion

9. **Age Rating:** 4+

10. **Content Rights:** Ensure all screenshots, assets, brand logos (Khaite, The Row, etc.) are either licensed or used for editorial/discovery purposes

### 7.1 Risk: Low Blog Traffic (Under 1,000 visits/month by month 3)

### 7.2 Risk: High Bounce Rate from Blog (60%+ bouncing without visiting landing page)

**Mitigation:**
1. Improve blog CTAs: More prominent, multiple CTAs per post
2. Add internal linking: Links to other blog posts (keep users on domain)
3. Add exit-intent popup: "Before you go, join the waitlist"
4. Improve blog design: Better readability, faster loading
5. A/B test headlines: Test emotional vs. educational headlines

### 7.3 Risk: Slow ASO Growth (Under 1,000 organic app installs/month)

**Mitigation:**
1. Expand keyword targeting: Add 10-15 new keywords to app store listing
2. A/B test screenshots: Test different designs, messaging

4. Increase review volume: Encourage early users to leave reviews (better ASO ranking)
