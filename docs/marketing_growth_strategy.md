# Rosier Marketing & Growth Strategy

**Target Audience:** Women 18-35, fashion-forward, micro-influencer followers, contemporary/premium brand shoppers
**Launch Timeline:** Summer 2026
**Goal:** Reach $10K MRR in 6 months post-launch
**Current Baseline:** 2,000+ waitlist subscribers

---

## Executive Summary

Rosier's growth strategy leverages three core channels: micro-influencer seeding (launch authenticity), content-driven SEO (organic discovery), and referral mechanics (viral coefficient). The strategy prioritizes early community-building and organic growth to establish brand credibility before scaling paid acquisition. Revenue model centers on affiliate commissions with B2B data monetization as secondary revenue stream.

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (3 Months Before Summer Launch)

### 1.1 Waitlist Building & Community Seeding

**Objective:** Build engaged audience of 10,000-15,000 waitlist subscribers by launch day, with 3-5% conversion to active users.

**Tactics:**

- **Micro-Influencer Seeding Program**
  - Partner with 20-30 micro-influencers (10K-50K followers) across TikTok and Instagram
  - Budget: $4,000-6,000 for early-stage seeding (product access + modest stipend)
  - Timeline: 8 weeks pre-launch
  - Content Types:
    - Day 1-2: Unboxing/first impressions (organic, raw)
    - Day 3-4: Styling with their own wardrobe ("how I'd wear this")
    - Day 5-7: Product deep-dive (fit, quality, brand story)
  - Platforms: TikTok (60%), Instagram Reels (30%), Stories (10%)
  - Expected reach: 500K-800K impressions, 3-5% CTR to landing page

- **Content Marketing Anchors**
  - Publish 3 "Style DNA" blog posts on landing page (target micro-influencer audiences):
    - "The Micro-Influencer's Guide to Finding Your Style DNA" (3,000 words)
    - "5 Contemporary Brands Dominating Fashion TikTok Right Now" (2,500 words)
    - "How to Shop Like You're On Fashion TikTok" (2,000 words)
  - Optimize for long-tail keywords (see SEO section below)
  - Repurpose into carousel posts, email snippets

- **Referral Incentive Structure**
  - Every waitlist signup includes unique referral link
  - Referrer benefits: 500 "Rosier coins" (points = store credit once app launches) per successful referral
  - Referred friend benefits: Early access tier (top 100 referrers get day-1 access vs. phased rollout)
  - Gamification: Public leaderboard showing top referrers (anonymized or by first name)
  - Expected viral coefficient: 0.3-0.5 (each user brings 0.3-0.5 new users organically)

- **Email Marketing Setup**
  - Use **Listmonk** (open-source email platform, free self-hosted)
  - Waitlist welcome sequence (3 emails over 2 weeks):
    - Email 1: Welcome + app preview video (day 0)
    - Email 2: Behind-the-scenes founder story + why Rosier exists (day 3)
    - Email 3: Early access tier explanation + referral incentive (day 7)
  - Weekly "Sneak Peek" campaign (Thursday 9 AM PT):
    - New brand spotlights, feature previews, influencer shoutouts
  - Target: 35-40% open rate (fashion-forward audience is engaged)

### 1.2 Social Strategy: TikTok & Instagram

**Platform Strategy:**

**TikTok (Primary Launch Channel)**
- Content pillars (weekly posting, 3x per week):
  - Trend takes: "Why micro-influencers are better at finding fashion than algorithms" (educational)
  - Behind-the-scenes: Curation process, team days, brand partnerships (authenticity)
  - User stories: Teaser content from early beta testers (social proof)
  - Duets/stitches: Engage with fashion TikTok creators, participate in trends
- Hashtag strategy: #NicheFashion, #MicroInfluencer, #FashionTikTok, #StyleDNA, #SwipeFashion
- Engagement: Reply to every comment in first 24 hours (community building)
- KPI: 50K-100K followers by launch, 5-8% engagement rate

**Instagram (Secondary Launch Channel)**
- Content pillars (5x per week):
  - Reels: Repurpose TikTok top performers (but Instagram-native edits)
  - Stories: Daily updates, polls, "This or That" style questions
  - Feed posts: Brand spotlights (1-2x per week), aesthetic content
  - Carousel posts: "5 Ways to Find Your Style DNA", brand deep-dives
- Hashtag strategy: More niche than TikTok (#SSENSEDrops, #ContemporaryFashion, #StyleFinder)
- Community building: Follow and engage with 100+ micro-influencers weekly (builds relationships pre-launch)
- KPI: 30K-50K followers by launch, 3-5% engagement rate

### 1.3 PR & Media Outreach

**Targets:**
- Fashion tech publications: BoF (Business of Fashion), The Glossary, ModaOperandi Insights
- Startup media: TechCrunch, Wired, The Verge (if relevant angle: AI for fashion discovery)
- Lifestyle/women's media: Refinery29 (if still active), Man Repeller, Vogue (pitches to fashion editors)
- Micro-influencer blogs/newsletters: Reach out to top micro-creators for early features

**Pitch Angles:**
1. "How AI is learning to think like a fashion-obsessed TikTok creator" (tech angle)
2. "The next frontier of fashion discovery: micro-influencer-powered recommendations" (trend angle)
3. "A founder's bet on niche fashion: Can Rosier replace your TikTok FYP?" (narrative angle)

**Timeline:** Start outreach 6 weeks before launch, aim for 3-5 features in the week of launch

### 1.4 Product Partnerships (Pre-Launch)

- **Brand Ambassador Program**
  - Recruit 5-8 "founding ambassadors" from waitlist (micro-influencers or community leaders)
  - Give exclusive early access 2 weeks pre-launch (for beta testing feedback)
  - Provide PR kit: Brand story, high-res assets, talking points
  - Compensation: $500-1,000 per ambassador + affiliate commission structure

## Phase 2: Launch (Week of Summer 2026)

### 2.1 Launch Day Blitz (Day 1)

**Timeline:** Tuesday or Wednesday (mid-week optimal for App Store algorithms)

**Day 0 Preparation:**
- App Store and Google Play listings fully optimized (see ASO section)
- Influencer content pre-scheduled for staggered release (10 AM PT, 1 PM PT, 4 PM PT, 7 PM PT)
- Email campaign queued: "Rosier is Live" to all 12,000+ waitlist subscribers
- Social media team on standby for community management

**Launch Day Actions:**
- 8 AM PT: Send launch email to waitlist ("It's here, get early access tier")
- 10 AM PT: TikTok influencers go live (staggered unboxing videos, first impressions)
- 12 PM PT: App appears in search results (ASO kicks in)
- 1 PM PT: Instagram Reels push (repurposed TikTok content)
- 3 PM PT: Product Hunt launch (see section 2.2)
- 5 PM PT: Twitter/X announcement with founder story
- 6 PM PT: Email #2 to non-converters: Social proof + app preview video

**Expected Day 1 Metrics:**
- Download target: 3,000-5,000 installs
- App Store ranking: Top 100 in Shopping category
- Waitlist conversion: 25-35%

### 2.2 Product Hunt Launch

**Strategy:**
- Launch on Tuesday morning PT (late afternoon UK, morning APAC)
- Prepare 4-6 weeks in advance: Build community presence, comment thoughtfully on 20+ products, earn credibility
- Maker presence: Founder or co-founder live in comments for first 6 hours

**Positioning:** "Tinder for niche fashion discovery powered by micro-influencer taste"

**Assets:**
- Product demo video (90 seconds)
- High-res GIF of swipe mechanics
- 3-4 customer testimonials (from beta testers)
- Clear problem/solution statement

**Goals:**
- Top 5 ranking in Product Hunt (shoot for #1 in Shopping)
- 1,000+ upvotes → 2,000-3,000 new installs
- Press coverage: "Product Hunt #1 fashion app" in founder bio

### 2.3 App Store Optimization (ASO) Launch

See Section 3 (ASO Strategy) for full details. Key actions:

- **App Title:** "Rosier - Swipe Fashion Discovery" (keyword-rich)
- **Keyword Targeting:** "niche fashion discovery", "fashion app", "designer clothes", "style DNA", "micro-influencer fashion"
- **Screenshots:** Swipe demo → style DNA → sale alerts (user journey)
- **Preview Video:** 30-sec loop of swipe mechanics + curation
- **Promotional Text:** Link to Product Hunt launch
- **Initial Download Target:** 5,000-8,000 installs in first week (to trigger ASO algorithm boost)

### 2.4 Referral Launch Mechanics

**Activation (In-App):**
- Launch referral tab within app (2nd tab, after discovery)
- One-click share: SMS, WhatsApp, Instagram DM, email, copy link
- Clear incentives displayed: "Get 500 Rosier coins per friend who joins"
- Leaderboard: Top 50 referrers shown (drives friendly competition)

**Incentive Structure:**
- Referrer: 500 Rosier coins per successful install
- Referred friend: 250 Rosier coins (free store credit)
- Coins = 1-for-1 with affiliate commission (redeemable for $ or brand partnerships)

**Target:** 10-15% of new users refer 1-2 friends in first 30 days (viral coefficient 0.1-0.15 = decent for social apps)

## Phase 3: Post-Launch (Months 1-6)

### 3.1 Content Marketing Engine

**Blog Strategy:**
- Host blog on separate subdomain (blog.rosier.app or rosier.app/style-guide)
- Publish 2 long-form pieces per month (2,500-4,000 words each), targeting SEO keywords
- Topics:
  - "The Ultimate Guide to Contemporary Fashion Brands" (evergreen, high-intent)
  - "Micro-Influencer Fashion: How to Shop Like Your Favorite Creators"
  - "Brand Spotlights": Deep-dives on Khaite, The Row, Jacquemus, Ganni (60+ content pieces over 6 months)
  - "Trend Reports": Seasonal trend analysis (differentiate Rosier as fashion authority)
  - "How to Build Your Style DNA" (product-focused, conversion funnel)

**Distribution:**
- Repurpose every blog post into:
  - 3-4 social media assets (carousel, Reel, TikTok)
  - 1 email newsletter excerpt
  - 1 short-form video (YouTube Shorts, TikTok)
- Email weekly: "Rosier Style Insights" (every Friday, 200-300 word digest)

**SEO Targets:** 50K-100K organic monthly visits by month 6

### 3.2 Community & Ambassador Program

**Expansion:**
- Scale founding ambassadors from 5-8 to 20-30 by month 3
- Commission structure:
  - Tier 1 (Micro, 10K-50K followers): 15-20% affiliate commission
  - Tier 2 (Mid, 50K-250K followers): 10-15% affiliate commission
  - Tier 3 (Macro, 250K+ followers): 5-10% + flat monthly retainer
- Provide monthly content calendar, product spotlights, affiliate links

**Retention Focus:**
- Monthly ambassador calls (product roadmap, feedback, Q&A)
- Dedicated Slack/Discord community for ambassadors
- Early access to new features, exclusive perks

**Targets:**
- 20+ active ambassadors by month 3
- 30-40% of month 3 new users from ambassador channels

### 3.3 Retention & Engagement Loops

**In-App Mechanics:**
- **Push Notifications** (use PostHog for targeting):
  - Personal recommendations: "We found 5 new pieces matching your style" (2x per week)
  - Sales alerts: "Price drop on your saved items" (1x per day, configurable)
  - Community: "New trend report: Spring Colors" (2x per month)
  - Referral: "You earned 500 coins! Share with friends" (onboarding + viral moments)
  - Personalization: Timing based on user engagement patterns (ML-driven)

- **Email Sequences**:
  - Week 1 (onboarding): "Complete your style profile" → 5 push types per user
  - Week 2: "You've swiped 500 items" → encourage saves + shares
  - Week 3: "Your first sale alert" → "Did you buy? Let us know your experience"
  - Week 4+: Weekly curated picks email (behavioral segmentation: budget, brands, categories)

- **Gamification**:
  - Daily streak: "Return to Rosier every day to keep your streak" (visual badge)
  - Achievement badges: "Style maven" (100+ saves), "Early adopter" (day 1 user), "Influencer" (5+ referrals)
  - Leaderboards: Top curators of the week (by saves, purchases, referrals)

**Target Metrics:**
- Week 1 retention: 45-50%
- Week 4 retention: 30-35%
- Month 3 retention: 20-25% (typical for mobile apps, okay for launch)
- Average session length: 8-12 minutes (swipe-based apps typically 5-10 min)
- Daily active users (DAU): 30-40% of monthly active users (MAU) by month 3

### 3.4 Paid Growth (Scaled Month 3+)

**Only after organic growth is validated (month 2-3).** Initial focus is organic + referral.

**Channels:**
1. **TikTok Ads:**
   - Budget: $1,000-2,000/week (month 3+)
   - Ad format: 15-second product demo, swipe mechanics, testimonials
   - Targeting: Women 18-35, interests in fashion, micro-influencers, design
   - CPA target: $3-5 per install (sustainable at $10K MRR goal)

2. **Instagram Ads:**
   - Budget: $500-1,000/week
   - Ad format: Reels (in-feed), carousel ads (brand spotlights)
   - Targeting: Lookalike audiences from early users + interest targeting
   - CPA target: $4-7 per install

3. **App Install Campaigns (Apple Search Ads + Google App Campaigns):**
   - Budget: $2,000-3,000/week
   - Focus on high-intent keywords: "fashion discovery app", "style app", "niche fashion"
   - CPA target: $2-3 per install

4. **TikTok Creator Marketplace:**
   - Partner with 10-20 creators (100K-500K followers) via official marketplace
   - Budget: $500-1,000 per creator per post
   - Target: 50K-100K views per creator, 1-2% CTR

**Monthly Growth Projection (Months 3-6):**
- Month 1: 5K-10K installs (organic + waitlist conversion)
- Month 2: 15K-25K installs (organic + referral + early ambassador channel)
- Month 3: 30K-50K installs (organic + referral + paid beginning)
- Month 4-6: 40K-80K installs/month (paid growth scaling)

## Phase 4: Retention & Monetization (Month 3+)

### 4.1 Affiliate Commission Tracking

**Setup:**
- Integrate with Kenshoo, Impact, or Refersion for affiliate tracking
- Track: Impressions, clicks, conversions, revenue per brand
- Commission structure: Standard 3-5% affiliate commission (negotiate with each brand/retailer)
- Monthly reconciliation: Ensure accuracy before payouts

**Optimization:**
- A/B test CTA placement: "Shop" button vs. product detail page vs. notification deep link
- Test notification frequency for sales alerts (too many = churn, too few = missed revenue)
- Personalize brand recommendations by user affinity (users who like Khaite → push Khaite sales first)

### 4.2 Expected Revenue (6-Month Target)

**Target: $10K MRR by month 6**

**Assumptions:**
- 200K total installs by month 6
- 20K monthly active users (10% retention)
- 10% conversion rate (users who click affiliate link)
- Average order value: $150
- Affiliate commission: 5%
- Revenue per conversion: $7.50

**Math:** 20K MAU × 10% conversion × $7.50 = $15K MRR (beating $10K target)

**Levers:**
- Increase user retention (push higher retention % → more MAU)
- Increase conversion rate (better product recommendations → more clicks)
- Increase AOV (feature wishlist, size recommendations, brand partnerships)
- Negotiate higher commissions (volume-based incentives with brands)

## Competitive Positioning

**vs. The Yes:**
- The Yes: Former Stitch Fix COO, AI-powered personalization, luxury positioning
- Rosier advantage: Swipe-based UX (TikTok-native), micro-influencer curation, affiliate-first monetization

**vs. Lyst:**
- Lyst: App-first Spotify-like discovery, $85M pre-IPO, established brand
- Rosier advantage: Smaller, more agile, micro-influencer authenticity, niche positioning (not all fashion)

**vs. Depop:**
- Depop: Social resale marketplace, sustainability focus, Gen Z native
- Rosier advantage: Primary retail + emerging brands, better monetization through affiliate model

**Rosier's Edge:**
- **Authenticity:** Micro-influencer curation vs. algorithmic (more trusted by Gen Z)
- **Niche focus:** Not all fashion (like Lyst), but contemporary + premium + emerging (differentiated market)
- **Unit economics:** Affiliate-first sustainable model (not dependent on venture capital for long-term sustainability)
- **Community:** Ambassador program ties micro-influencers into product development (active feedback loop)

---

## Risk Mitigation

1. **Influencer Dependence:** If key influencers don't convert well, pivot budget to paid ads (TikTok Ads, Instagram Ads) by month 2.
2. **Affiliate Commission Volatility:** Negotiate minimum commission rates with top retailers; diversify across brands to reduce dependency on single partner.
3. **Low User Retention:** Implement cohort analysis (PostHog) to identify drop-off points; prioritize retention features (notifications, gamification) over new user acquisition.
4. **Algorithm Changes (App Store/TikTok):** Diversify channels early (email, blog, community) to reduce platform dependency.
5. **Competitive Entry:** Move fast on community-building and brand partnerships; switching costs are low, so lock in early users through loyalty (referral rewards, ambassador relationships).
