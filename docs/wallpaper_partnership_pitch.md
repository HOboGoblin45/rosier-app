## How It Works: The Wallpaper Reveal Mechanic

### User Experience

Rosier's core interaction is simple and delightful:

1. **Initial State:** User sees a fashion product card (clothing, accessories, footwear) on the screen
2. **The Swipe:** User swipes left/right to move through product discovery
3. **The Reveal:** As the card swipes away, the surface beneath reveals a wallpaper pattern
4. **Archetype Matching:** The pattern shown is matched to the user's style archetype (based on her swipe history, follower preferences, interaction patterns)

### Why This Works

- **High Engagement:** The reveal creates a micro-moment of delight and surprise
- **Brand Impressionism:** Each wallpaper appearance is 2-3 seconds of active attention (vs. banner ads: 1.2 seconds)
- **Aesthetic Fit:** The pattern reinforces the user's sense of personal taste—she sees wallpaper that aligns with her fashion choices
- **Archetype Resonance:** Different users with different style identities see different patterns, creating deep relevance

### The Data You Get

Every impression is trackable:
- **Pattern impressions** per user session
- **Time on pattern** (engagement intensity)
- **User demographic data** (age, location, estimated income, follower count)
- **Fashion context** (what product category was user browsing when pattern was shown?)
- **Archetype affinity** (which user style segments engage most with your pattern?)
- **Downstream behavior** (click-through to brand info, if offered)
