## TONE & VOICE NOTES

**DO:**
- Be authentic, talk like a real founder
- Use "we" (team) not "I"
- Ask for feedback, show you listen
- Celebrate community wins
- Make honest admissions ("we're still building X")

**DON'T:**
- Use corporate jargon ("synergize", "leverage")
- Oversell or exaggerate
- Ignore comments/DMs
- Post inspirational quotes (cringe)
- Act like you have all the answers

**Tone:** Friend explaining a cool app to another friend, not a brand manager selling to you.
